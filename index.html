Campus life

ITI CENTRAL SCHOOL

ITI Central School is a Central Board of Secondary Education school located in Dooravani Nagar, Bangalore, India. The pre-primary and early years learning programme at ITI1 is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, Chennai Public School has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Smart Class – Interactive Digital classroom:

ITI Central School was one of the first schools in India to have smart boards in all the classrooms and also a 5.1 theatre system in all classrooms.

Sports & games
Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Alphard House, Canopus House, Diadem House and Plasma House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
